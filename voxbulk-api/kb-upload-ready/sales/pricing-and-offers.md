## Trial and signup

- Primary close: **free trial signup** via personalised link (email + WhatsApp after call).
- Appointment / recall plans: typically **15-day free trial** on subscription plans (confirm current plan in admin if changed).
- Survey and interview offers: trial or credit bundles as configured in offer templates (survey contacts / interview sessions included after signup).
- No credit card required to start trial unless product policy changes — state what the current offer template says at send time.
