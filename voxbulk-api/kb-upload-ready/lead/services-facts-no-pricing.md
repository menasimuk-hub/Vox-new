## What VOXBULK is

VOXBULK is an AI voice and messaging platform for businesses that need to recover appointments, reach customers, run surveys, and screen candidates by phone and WhatsApp.

## Who it is for

- Dental and medical practices (recall, reactivation, reminders)
- Opticians, beauty, wellness clinics
- Recruitment and HR teams (interview screening)
- Any business that makes outbound calls or WhatsApp messages at scale

## Core capabilities

- **Outbound AI voice calls** — natural conversations, booking, qualification
- **WhatsApp** — templates, follow-ups, two-way chat
- **SMS** — where configured
- **Surveys** — collect feedback by voice or message
- **AI interviews** — structured candidate screening
- **Dashboard** — campaigns, transcripts, outcomes, admin control
