# VoxBulk — WhatsApp Utility Survey Templates
**140 templates across 7 industries**
All templates comply with Meta WhatsApp Utility category rules:
- Non-promotional, no upsells or offers
- Specific to a user's visit, booking or interaction
- Max 3 buttons per message, plain text only, max 20 characters each
- Emoji in body only
